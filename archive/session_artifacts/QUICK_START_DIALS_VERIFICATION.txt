================================================================================
                          WHAT EACH DIAL DOES
================================================================================

MIN_GENRE_SIMILARITY [0.0 - 1.0]
  Controls: Genre coherence of candidate pool
  Default: 0.20 (lenient)
  Effect: Excludes candidates with genre_sim < threshold (hard gate in dynamic)

  Lenient (0.15):   Allows diverse genres
  Balanced (0.30):  Moderate genre coherence
  Strict (0.50):    Forces similar genres only

GENRE_METHOD
  Controls: How genre similarity is computed
  Options: "ensemble", "cosine", "weighted_jaccard"
  Effect: Different methods → different candidate rankings → different playlists

  ensemble:         0.6*cosine + 0.4*jaccard (recommended)
  cosine:           Float similarity on smoothed genres
  weighted_jaccard: Binary overlap on raw genres

TRANSITION_STRICTNESS
  Controls: Quality of track-to-track transitions
  Options: "baseline", "strictish", "strict", "lenient"
  Effect: Higher floors force smooth transitions, smaller effective pool

  baseline (0.30):  Natural transitions, large candidate pool
  strictish (0.85): Smooth transitions, medium pool
  strict (0.95):    Extremely smooth transitions, tiny pool
